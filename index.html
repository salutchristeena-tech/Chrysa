Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

What is Chrysa?

Chrysa is an AI marketing command centre. It connects the tools you already run — GA4, HubSpot, SEMrush, Klaviyo, Modash and more — and reads them as one system, so every past campaign, live project and future calendar lives under one roof.

Does Chrysa replace my existing tools?

No. Chrysa sits above your stack, not instead of it. Your dashboards keep doing what they do — Chrysa reads them together, so the fragments become one answer instead of ten tabs.

How is this different from asking ChatGPT?

A general chatbot guesses from whatever you paste into it. Chrysa answers from your connected, live stack — and cites the source of every number, so you can defend the answer in a budget meeting.

How is Chrysa different from enterprise suites like Sprinklr?

Enterprise platforms are built for hundred-person marketing organisations, with contracts and onboarding to match. Chrysa is built for the person who is the marketing department — connected in minutes, from $29 a month.

Where does my data go?

Chrysa connects to your tools read-only and your workspace stays yours. Disconnect any source at any time.

What does early access include?

The beta dashboard today, a founding-member rate locked for as long as you stay subscribed, and a real say in what ships next. Monthly billing, cancel anytime.
