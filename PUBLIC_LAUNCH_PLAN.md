# Chrysa First 10 Users Plan

## Goal

Get 10 people to use the Chrysa landing page, submit their website URL, join the waitlist, and give feedback on whether the roadmap is useful.

## Ideal First Users

Start with people who feel reporting pain often:

- founders running their own marketing
- restaurant and hospitality owners
- marketing directors
- PR and communications leads
- small agencies
- startup operators
- consultants managing multiple client reports

## Offer

Use this simple promise:

> I am building Chrysa, an AI command centre for marketing teams. It connects reports, campaign data, PR, influencers, content, budgets, and ROI into one dashboard. I am inviting 10 early users to test the website roadmap and tell me what they would need to trust this as their marketing command centre.

## What To Ask Them To Do

1. Visit the public Chrysa link.
2. Join the waitlist.
3. Enter their website URL.
4. Read the roadmap.
5. Reply with:
   - what felt accurate
   - what was missing
   - which integrations they need first
   - whether they would use this weekly

## LinkedIn Launch Post

I am building Chrysa.

Marketing teams are drowning in scattered reports: Google Analytics, Meta Ads, PR coverage, influencer updates, agency decks, content performance, budgets, and ROI.

Chrysa is designed to become one command centre for every marketing decision.

The first MVP lets you:

- join the early-access list
- enter your website URL
- get a roadmap of what is lacking
- see which campaigns to work on next
- understand how connected marketing data could become clear action

I am looking for 10 early users: founders, marketers, PR teams, agencies, restaurants, hospitality brands, and startups.

If you want to try it, comment or DM me and I will send the link.

## Follow-Up Questions

Ask every tester:

- What part of the roadmap felt most useful?
- What felt generic or missing?
- Which tool should Chrysa connect first?
- What report do you hate building every week or month?
- Would you pay for this if it saved you 3 hours per week?
- What would make you trust the recommendations?

## Success Criteria

The first launch is working if:

- 10 people submit the form
- 5 people test the website roadmap
- 3 people agree to a feedback call
- 2 people say they would pay for a focused version
- you know the top 2 integrations people want first

## Next Build After 10 Users

Build only what the first users ask for repeatedly:

1. Persistent signup database.
2. Real website analysis.
3. Exportable roadmap PDF.
4. First integration.
5. Admin lead dashboard.
6. Email follow-up automation.
